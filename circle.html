Built for real property professionals

COHO Circles is a peer community for experienced and engaged property professionals.

Each Circle is a small, private WhatsApp group - 30 members maximum. No algorithm deciding what you see. No advertising. No lurkers. Just a tight group of people who are passionate about the industry.

What it's for

  • Asking the questions AI doesn't have the experience to weigh in on
  • Sharing what's actually working - and what isn't
  • Getting a second opinion from someone who's been there
  • Honest conversation about the industry

What it isn't

  • An advertising channel
  • A place to lurk
  • Free advice on tap with nothing given back

How it works

1

Apply

Fill in the short form below. Takes two minutes.

2

Join the waiting list

We don't add people to just any group. We wait until there's the right space in a group that suits you.

3

Get invited

When a spot opens, you'll receive a WhatsApp invite to your Circle.

4

Contribute

It takes each member to build and maintain a community. Those who don't engage will give up their spot to someone on the waiting list.

How we keep it worth being part of

Communities only work when people contribute. Every member who joins takes a spot that someone on the waiting list is waiting for.

We ask one thing: show up. Share something. Respond when you can.

The 30-day rule

If a month passes with no activity from you, your spot returns to the waiting list automatically - no exceptions. You'll get a nudge a few days before if you've gone quiet, but after 30 days the spot is gone.

No advertising

This isn't a place to sell. It's a place to form a community with peers. Please don't use this as a channel to share your content, along with multiple other channels.

To rejoin, you go back to the bottom of the waiting list.

That rule exists to protect the quality of the group for everyone in it.

Apply to join

Applications are open to active COHO users only. We'll review your application and be in touch when there's a suitable space.
